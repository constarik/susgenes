sus.genes — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

What we collect

sus.genes accesses your Telegram display name and profile photo solely to personalize the in-game interface. This data is provided by the Telegram Mini Apps API and is not stored on our servers.

Game progress (balance, round number) is stored in Telegram CloudStorage, which is tied to your Telegram account and managed by Telegram.

What we don't collect

We do not collect, store, or share your phone number, contacts, messages, or any personal data beyond what is described above.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Telegram Mini Apps Analytics SDK to understand general usage patterns (page views, session duration). No personally identifiable information is collected by these services.

Payments

Optional purchases are processed entirely through Telegram's built-in payment system (Telegram Stars). We do not collect or store any payment details. All transactions are handled by Telegram.

Third-party services

The game runs on GitHub Pages and uses the Telegram Bot API and Telegram Stars payment system. These services have their own privacy policies.

Contact

Questions? Reach us via @susgenes_bot on Telegram.
